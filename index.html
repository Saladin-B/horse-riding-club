Welcome to Horse Riding Club

Experience the thrill of horse riding with us. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rider, we have something for everyone.

Services We Provide

Lunge

Lunge training for all levels, one-to-one lesson with instructor in the arena. 12 lunges per session, before going solo.

Group Lessons

A guided group riding lesson where riders learn and improve essential horse riding skills together. Sessions focus on balance, control, communication, and technique while riding, and supervised setting.

Forest Hacks

Enjoy a relaxing guided hack through peaceful countryside, designed for both group and solo riders. Suitable for all experience levels, this ride offers a calm, and enjoyable way to explore nature on horseback.

Pony Rides

A fun and beginner-friendly pony riding session where riders enjoy a calm, guided ride. Perfect for building confidence and creating a positive first riding experience.
